Frequently Asked Questions

How does location detection work?

We use your device's location services to automatically detect your current location. You can also manually update your location at any time.

Can I create my own rooms?

Yes! While our AI creates rooms automatically, you can also create custom rooms for specific topics or interests.

How private are the conversations?

All room conversations are private to the members of that room. We prioritize user privacy and data security.
